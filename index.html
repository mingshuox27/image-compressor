Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image compressor really free?
Yes, completely free with no limits. Compress as many images as you want, as often as you need.
Can I compress multiple images at once?
Yes! Simply select multiple images and click "Compress All". You can then download them individually or as a ZIP file.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly. Your images never leave your device — 100% private and secure.
What image formats are supported?
We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP. You can also convert between formats during compression (e.g., PNG to JPEG).
Can I resize images while compressing?
Yes. Use the "Max Width" option to resize images proportionally. Choose from preset widths or keep the original size.
How much can I reduce file size?
Typically 50-80% reduction depending on the quality setting and image type. At 80% quality, the visual difference is usually unnoticeable.
Is there a file size or quantity limit?
Free version: max 20MB per file, unlimited files. Pro version removes the 20MB limit for larger images.
